# Quiz Feature - User Guide

## Team: RNA

---

## Overview

The Quiz feature enables teachers to automatically generate personalized quizzes from course materials using AI, and allows students to take these quizzes to assess their learning progress. This guide provides step-by-step instructions for both teachers and students.

---

## For Teachers

### Creating a Quiz with AI

#### Step 1: Navigate to Quiz Generation

1. Log in to CourseWise as a teacher
2. Click on **"Quiz Management"** in the teacher portal navigation
3. Click the **"Generate Quiz"** button (green button with plus icon)

#### Step 2: Configure Quiz Parameters

Fill out the quiz configuration form:

**Basic Information:**
- **Course** (required): Select the course you want to generate a quiz for
- **Quiz Title** (required): Enter a descriptive title (e.g., "Python Fundamentals Quiz")
- **Description** (optional): Add a brief description of what the quiz covers

**Quiz Settings:**
- **Number of Questions**: Choose how many questions to generate (1-50, recommended 5-15)
- **Difficulty Level**: Select the difficulty:
  - **Easy**: Basic recall and understanding
  - **Medium**: Application and analysis
  - **Hard**: Synthesis and evaluation
- **Specific Topics** (optional): Enter comma-separated topics to focus on (e.g., "Variables, Loops, Functions")
  - Leave empty to cover all course content

#### Step 3: Generate Quiz

1. Click **"Generate Quiz with AI"** button
2. Wait for the AI to analyze your course materials and generate questions (15-30 seconds)
3. You'll see a loading spinner with "Generating Questions..." message
4. Once complete, a success message will appear

#### Step 4: Review Generated Questions

The preview panel on the right shows all generated questions:

- **Overview**: Number of questions, total points, difficulty level
- **Question List**: Expand each accordion item to review:
  - Question text
  - Question type (multiple-choice, true-false, short-answer)
  - Answer options (for multiple-choice)
  - Correct answer
  - Explanation
  - Point value
  - Topic covered

**Quality Check**: Verify that questions are:
- âœ… Relevant to your course content
- âœ… Clear and unambiguous
- âœ… Appropriate for the difficulty level
- âœ… Have accurate correct answers

#### Step 5: Save Quiz

1. Review all questions in the preview
2. Click **"Save Quiz"** button at the bottom
3. Quiz is saved and you're redirected to the Quiz Management page
4. Your new quiz now appears in the list

### Managing Your Quizzes

#### Viewing Your Quiz List

1. Navigate to **"Quiz Management"** in the teacher portal
2. See all your created quizzes with:
   - Quiz title and difficulty badge
   - Course name, number of questions, total points
   - Description
   - Topics covered
   - **Statistics**: Total attempts, average score, completion rate

#### Filtering Quizzes

Use the filter dropdowns to narrow down your quiz list:
- **Filter by Course**: Show only quizzes for a specific course
- **Filter by Difficulty**: Show only easy, medium, or hard quizzes

#### Viewing Quiz Details

1. Click **"View Details"** on any quiz card
2. See comprehensive analytics:
   - **Overview Statistics**: Total attempts, average score, questions, points
   - **Quiz Details**: Description, topics, created date
   - **Question Performance**: How students performed on each question
     - Expand questions to see correct/incorrect counts
     - Identify questions that are too easy or too hard
   - **Student Attempts**: Table showing all student submissions
     - Student name, score, percentage, completion date, status

#### Understanding Quiz Analytics

**Overall Statistics:**
- **Total Attempts**: How many students have completed the quiz
- **Average Score**: Mean percentage score across all attempts
- **Questions**: Total number of questions in the quiz
- **Total Points**: Maximum points available

**Question Performance:**
- **Green badge (70%+)**: Most students answered correctly (easy question)
- **Yellow badge (50-69%)**: Mixed results (appropriate difficulty)
- **Red badge (<50%)**: Most students struggled (consider reviewing this content)

**Student Attempts Table:**
- **Score**: Points earned out of maximum
- **Percentage**: Score as a percentage (color-coded by performance)
- **Status**: Completed or in-progress

## For Students

### Finding Available Quizzes

#### Step 1: Navigate to Quizzes

1. Log in to CourseWise as a student
2. Click on **"Quizzes"** or **"Available Quizzes"** in the student portal navigation

#### Step 2: Browse Quizzes

View all available quizzes with the following information:
- **Quiz Title** and difficulty badge
- **Course Name**
- **Description**: What the quiz covers
- **Quiz Info**:
  - Number of questions
  - Total points
  - Estimated time
- **Your Status**: Not Started, In Progress, or Completed with score

#### Step 3: Filter Quizzes

Use filters to find specific quizzes:
- **Filter by Course**: Show quizzes for a specific course
- **Filter by Difficulty**: Show easy, medium, or hard quizzes
- **Filter by Status**: Show not-started or completed quizzes

---

### Taking a Quiz

#### Step 1: Start Quiz

1. Find the quiz you want to take
2. Click **"Start Quiz"** button (or "Continue Quiz" if in progress)
3. You'll see the quiz taking interface

#### Step 2: Answer Questions

**Navigation:**
- Questions are shown one at a time
- Progress bar shows your current position (e.g., "Question 2 of 5")
- Count of answered questions is displayed

**Question Types:**

1. **Multiple Choice**: Select one answer from 4 options
   - Click the radio button next to your chosen answer

2. **True/False**: Determine if the statement is true or false
   - Click True or False radio button

3. **Short Answer**: Type your answer in the text box
   - Enter your answer carefully (spelling matters!)

**Navigation Buttons:**
- **Previous**: Go back to previous question
- **Next**: Move to next question
- **Submit Quiz**: Appears on last question when ready to submit

#### Step 3: Submit Quiz

1. After answering questions, click **"Submit Quiz"**
2. Review the confirmation dialog:
   - Shows how many questions you've answered
   - **Warning**: If you have unanswered questions
3. Click **"Submit"** to confirm or **"Cancel"** to continue answering
4. Wait for submission to process (usually instant)

**Note**: Once submitted, you cannot change your answers!

#### Step 4: View Results

After submission, you're automatically redirected to the results page.

### Understanding Your Results

#### Performance Summary

**Score Display:**
- Large percentage circle showing your score (e.g., 80%)
- Points earned out of maximum (e.g., 8/10 pts)
- Performance message:
  - 90%+: "Excellent!"
  - 75-89%: "Great job!"
  - 60-74%: "Good effort!"
  - <60%: "Keep practicing!"

**Statistics:**
- **Questions**: Total number of questions
- **Correct**: How many you got right (green)
- **Incorrect**: How many you got wrong (red)

**Action Buttons:**
- **Retake Quiz**: Start the quiz again from scratch
- **Back to Quizzes**: Return to quiz list

#### Question Review

**Accordion List:**
Each question is shown in an expandable section with:
- âœ… Green checkmark: Correct answer
- âŒ Red X: Incorrect answer
- Points earned out of possible points

**Expand a Question to See:**
1. **Full Question Text**: The complete question
2. **Your Answer**: What you selected/typed (highlighted)
   - Green background: Correct
   - Red background: Incorrect
3. **Correct Answer**: The right answer (if you got it wrong)
4. **Explanation**: Why this is the correct answer
   - Read carefully to learn from mistakes!
5. **Metadata**: Topic, question type, points

**Learning from Mistakes:**
- Pay special attention to questions you got wrong
- Read the explanations to understand the concept
- Note which topics you need to review
- Consider retaking the quiz after studying

### Retaking Quizzes

You can retake quizzes as many times as you want!

**To Retake:**
1. From the results page, click **"Retake Quiz"**
   - OR -
2. From the quiz list, click **"Retake Quiz"** on a completed quiz

**Note**: Each attempt is independent. You start fresh each time.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### For Teachers

**Q: How long does quiz generation take?**
A: Usually 15-30 seconds, depending on course content length and number of questions.

**Q: Can I edit generated questions?**
A: Not in the current version. Review questions before saving. Future versions will support editing.

**Q: What if the AI generates irrelevant questions?**
A: Don't save the quiz. Try again with more specific topics or check that your course materials are comprehensive.

**Q: Can students see correct answers during the quiz?**
A: No, correct answers are only shown after quiz submission.

**Q: How is the score calculated?**
A: Each question has a point value. Correct answers earn those points. Total score is sum of points earned.

**Q: Can I delete a quiz?**
A: Not yet implemented in this version. Future versions will support quiz deletion.

### For Students

**Q: Can I pause a quiz and come back later?**
A: Yes! Your answers are auto-saved. You can close the page and resume later.

**Q: How many times can I retake a quiz?**
A: Unlimited! Feel free to retake quizzes to improve your understanding.

**Q: Are quizzes timed?**
A: No, take as much time as you need to answer thoughtfully.

**Q: What if I accidentally submit without answering all questions?**
A: You'll get a warning, but you can proceed. Unanswered questions receive 0 points.

**Q: Can I see other students' scores?**
A: No, only you and your teacher can see your scores.

**Q: Do quiz scores affect my grade?**
A: That depends on your teacher's grading policy. Check with them!

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Issue**: "Quiz not found" error
- **Solution**: The quiz may have been deleted. Go back to quiz list.

**Issue**: Quiz generation fails
- **Solution**: 
  - Make sure you selected a course
  - Check that the course has uploaded materials
  - Try reducing the number of questions
  - Reload the page and try again

**Issue**: Can't submit quiz
- **Solution**:
  - Check your internet connection
  - Make sure you answered at least one question
  - Try refreshing the page
  - If answers are saved, they'll be restored

**Issue**: Results not showing
- **Solution**:
  - Wait a few seconds for loading
  - Refresh the page
  - Check the URL is correct
  - Go back to quiz list and find your attempt
